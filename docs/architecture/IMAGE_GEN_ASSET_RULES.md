# RPG Skeleton - Image Gen Asset Rules

No generated assets are active after the purge. Future Image Gen assets may be introduced only through this governed workflow.

## Rules
1. Generate atomic assets, not full maps or mixed atlases.
2. Keep terrain tiles separate from object sprites and actor sprites.
3. Record prompt, source file, processed file, world size, pivot, footprint, collision, and approval screenshot.
4. Import through metadata, not ad hoc scene edits.
5. Verify scale, collision, layering, readability, and tiling in a Godot screenshot before approval.
6. Do not promote an asset to active content until it passes the visual gate.
